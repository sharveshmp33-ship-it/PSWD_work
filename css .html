The Power of Money

What Is Money?

Money is anything that is widely accepted as a medium of exchange.

Money helps people buy goods, pay for services, save for the future, and invest for growth.

Functions of Money

  • Medium of Exchange
  • Store of Value
  • Unit of Account
  • Standard of Deferred Payment

Types of Money (Table)

Type of Money Description
Cash Physical currency like coins and notes
Digital Money Money stored electronically (UPI, bank apps)
Bank Deposits Money stored in bank accounts
Cryptocurrency Digital currency based on blockchain technology

Money is powerful — but managing it wisely is even more important!
