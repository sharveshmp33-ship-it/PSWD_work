All About Money

1. What is Money?

Money is a medium of exchange that allows people to buy goods and services. It has been used for thousands of years in different forms — coins, paper notes, and now digital currency.

2. Types of Money

  • Coins
  • Paper Notes
  • Digital Money
  • Cryptocurrency

3. How People Use Money

  1. To buy goods and services
  2. To save for the future
  3. To invest and earn more
  4. To pay debts and taxes
